Ashwada in Hinduism - the concept of blessing
Blessings in Hinduism. Ashirvada is deeply rooted in the Hindi word for respecting the beauty of the elderly. 
The habit of touching elders feet for blessings is common, and this traditional parenting aspect can be rooted in their scriptures, Ihatihasas, Puranas and Dharmashastras.
The essence of India can be used in the application to impart this blessing
